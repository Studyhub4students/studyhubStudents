Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic account credentials and academic metadata directly provided by you, including:

  • **Registration Data**: Name, phone number, role (student/educator/admin), and securely hashed passwords.
  • **Support Data**: Information provided when submitting support or forgot-password tickets.
  • **Usage Data**: Documents uploaded, resource likes, and chatbot conversation history (to maintain context).

2. How We Use Information

We utilize your data strictly to operate and support Studyhub, specifically to:

  • Manage authentication, permissions, and session validation.
  • Maintain academic leaderboards (displaying educator name, contributions, and cumulative likes).
  • Process and route academic chatbot responses.
  • Dispatch system notifications and direct administrative messages.

3. Data Storage and Third-Party API Sharing

Your data is stored securely in our MongoDB collections and Cloudinary storage (for direct PDF uploads). Chatbot prompts are shared temporarily with third-party LLM inference services (Hugging Face Inference API and Puter AI) to generate responses. We do not sell or monetize your personal details.

4. Data Security

Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing before database insertion. Session traffic is authorized via JSON Web Tokens (JWT) stored in your local browser container. Please log out when using public computers to prevent unauthorized access.

5. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to align with system features. Significant updates will be accompanied by an announcement banner in the platform.
